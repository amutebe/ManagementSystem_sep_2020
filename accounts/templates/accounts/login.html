Introduction:
AMSS is a management systems software owned by Andy Systems,
an independent management consultancy in implementation, training and
auditing of management systems related to ISO standards including ISO 9001,
ISO 27001 and ISO 20000. The software’s first and current release or version
is 1.0.0 with a purpose of providing simplicity in the implementation and
operation of your management systems and processes and provides for activity
Planning, Tracking, Reporting and Document management.
Software Features:
  • Risk Assessment
  • Performance Evaluation
  • Document Management
  • Corrective Action Tracking
  • Task Management
  • Training Evaluation
